Exist

Why would I want death
if it weren't for living

why would I want air
if it weren't for breathing

why would I chase dreams
if it weren't already caught

on my bed, as I weave
through the tangle of a melody
through the strings of a violin
through the beams of a stairwell
why would I

if it weren't for you?
