I want to live

by Ronnel Bermas

Because to live means
To conjure stories out of silence.

Because to conjure stories means
To cast spirits out of hibernation.

Because I want to grasp my soul like it was my own—
Walk down the throbbing road and be lost in remembering.

Or perhaps I just want to bite on someone's cheek.

And camp somewhere safe.
       listen to the bubbling of cooking.
       run as though I had all the hours I need.
       let the wind sting my face like life greeting me

Without saying goodbye.
